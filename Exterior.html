EN | This site was uncharacterized. A plot with two private houses and in between the remaining space. The project consists in defining connections with the existing buildings, grading spaces, providing access to different levels and giving meaning to the landscape layout. The existing buildings without interest or quality were demolished, and a car parking is set in its place, separated from the place dedicated to people. At the renovated kitchen building level, two areas - separated by a flowerbed - are defined from drawing connections of existing buildings. The hard landscape design express the defined alignments. The gardens complement the set and will provide privacy from the adjacent plot in the future.
